FAQs
What is Schedulify? v

Schedulify is a centralized web-based appointment system designed specifically for students to book consultation slots with lecturers. It replaces manual scheduling with an organized, digital dashboard to improve efficiency and reduce waiting times.

How do I book an appointment? v

Once you log in as a student, simply browse the available lecturers, select your required service (Consultation or Counselling), choose an open time slot from their schedule, and submit your request. You can track the status of your booking directly from your dashboard.

Can I cancel my appointment? v

Yes, you can cancel your appointment through the system. However, standard cancellations must be made at least three working days before the scheduled time. For last-minute changes, you must use the Emergency Request feature and provide valid supporting evidence.

How will I know if my appointment is approved? v

The status of your appointment will be updated in real-time on your dashboard. You will see labels such as Pending (awaiting lecturer approval), Approved (confirmed), or Rejected/Completed. In-system notifications will also remind you before your session starts.

Can lecturers manage their own availability? v

Absolutely. Lecturers have a dedicated module where they can set their available days and hours. They also have the authority to approve, reschedule, or decline appointment requests based on their academic schedule.

Is there a history of my past appointments? v

Yes, Schedulify includes an Appointment History feature. Once an appointment is marked as Completed, it is archived in your history tab, allowing both students and staff to keep track of past consultations and feedback for future reference.
